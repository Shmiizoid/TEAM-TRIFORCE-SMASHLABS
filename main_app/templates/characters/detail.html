GameCube Controller Layout:

  • Control Stick:
    Move your character.
  • C-Stick (Right Analog Stick):
    Perform Smash Attacks in the corresponding direction.
  • A Button:
    Perform Normal Attacks (Jab, Tilt Attacks, etc.).
  • B Button:
    Perform Special Attacks.
  • X and Y Buttons:
    Jump.
  • L Button:
    Grab/Shield (Press to grab, hold to shield).
  • R Button:
    Shield/Grab (Press to shield, hold to grab).
  • Z Button:
    Perform a Grab/Throw.
  • D-Pad:
    Used for taunts in some games.

Additional Notes:

You can perform different attacks based on how you tilt the control stick and press the A button (Tilt Attacks).


The strength of Smash Attacks can vary depending on how long you hold the C-Stick in the corresponding direction.


The controls may vary slightly between different Super Smash Bros. games, but the basic layout remains mostly consistent.
